# Grid
### Grid system using flexbox.

## Equal Width:
Passing nothing into `<Grid>`, you will get equal width columns based on the number of elements as its children.

<Playground>
	<Grid>
		<Div>col #1</Div>
		<Div>col #2</Div>
	</Grid>
	‌‌
	<Grid>
		<Div>col #1</Div>
		<Div>col #2</Div>
		<Div>col #3</Div>
	</Grid>
	‌‌
	<Grid>
		<Div>col #1</Div>
		<Div>col #2</Div>
		<Div>col #3</Div>
		<Div>col #4</Div>
	</Grid>
</Playground>

## Specific Width/Columns:
`<Grid>` takes 3 different arrays corresponding to different resolutions (`itemWidthsSmall`, `itemWidthsMedium`, and `itemWidthsLarge`), the elements of which are integers that act as columns. If you want columns to be equally sized at a specific resolution, simply leave out that resolution in question.

<Playground>
	<Grid
		itemWidthsLarge={[7, 5]}
		itemWidthsMedium={[8, 4]}
		itemWidthsSmall={[9, 3]}
	>
		<Div>Large: 7 cols — Medium: 8 cols — Small: 9 cols</Div>
		<Div>Large: 5 cols — Medium: 4 cols — Small: 3 cols</Div>
	</Grid>
	‌
	<Grid
		itemWidthsLarge={[8, 4]}
		itemWidthsMedium={[3, 9]}
	>
		<Div>Large: 8 cols — Medium: 3 cols — Small: 6 cols</Div>
		<Div>Large: 4 cols — Medium: 9 cols — Small: 6 cols</Div>
	</Grid>
	‌
	<Grid
		itemWidthsLarge={[4, 8]}
		itemWidthsMedium={[8, 4]}
	>
		<Div>Large: 4 cols — Medium: 8 cols — Small: 6 cols</Div>
		<Div>Large: 8 cols — Medium: 4 cols — Small: 6 cols</Div>
	</Grid>
</Playground>

## Stacking:
`stackSmall`, `stackMedium`, and `stackLarge` will stack the elements top-to-bottom at full-width.

<Playground>
	<Grid
		stackSmall
		stackMedium
		stackLarge
	>
		<Div>col #1</Div>
		<Div>col #2</Div>
	</Grid>
</Playground>


## Swapping:
`swapSmall`, `swapMedium`, and `swapLarge` will reverse the order of elements at the specified resolution.

<Playground>
	<Grid
		swapSmall
		swapMedium
		swapLarge
	>
		<Div>1st in source</Div>
		<Div>2nd in source</Div>
	</Grid>
</Playground>

## Gutter Widths:
`gutterSmall`, `gutterMedium`, and `gutterLarge` takes a named spacing string and adjusts the gutter width accordingly. Defaults to `x-small`.
